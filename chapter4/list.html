Pigs
Pig (Sus domesticus), also known as swine or hog, is a domesticated, omnivorous, even-toed hoofed mammal. It belongs to the family Suidae and is closely related to the wild boar (Sus scrofa), with some authorities classifying it as a subspecies (Sus scrofa domesticus) and others as a distinct species (Sus domesticus). Pigs are highly intelligent, social animals with a reputation for cleanliness—despite their mud-wallowing habit, which helps regulate body temperature and protect against parasites.
